Steps to Submit the Camera-Ready Paper and Online Registration

  1. Register online at https://scrs.in/conference/aic2024/page/registration_fee . Click the “Click Here to Register” link on the registration page. You’ll be forwarded to Conference Services for the payment of the registration fee. Select AIC 2024 | IEEE World Conference on Applied Intelligence and Computing and complete payment after filling in the required details.
  2. After completing the payment, you’ll receive a confirmation email with an invoice. Check your spam or other folders if not found in the primary inbox. This completes the payment process.
  3. Convert your paper to the IEEE template using the following:
    • US letter (DOC, 30 KB) Updated Jan 2019
    • LaTeX Template Instructions (PDF, 63 KB) [Use the conference mode.]
    • Template (ZIP, 700 KB) Updated October 2019
    • LaTeX Bibliography Files (ZIP, 309 KB)

    Overleaf:

    When using Overleaf, the IEEE template is available at https://www.overleaf.com/gallery/tagged/ieee-official .

  4. Go to https://mss7.scrs.in/ for camera-ready paper submission.
  5. Enter your Paper ID and password. The password is formed by combining the first four characters of the paper title with the paper ID.

    Example: If the paper title is “Advances in AI” and paper ID is “212”, the password will be ADVA212.
  6. Upload a .zip folder containing:
    • PDF of the revised paper
    • Source files (Word or LaTeX)
    • Reviewers’ comments response
    • Payment proof (invoice)
    • SCRS/IEEE Membership Certificate (if registered)

Note: The last date for camera-ready paper submission, payment of registration fee, and online registration is July 14, 2024.

For any queries, write to “General Chairs, AIC 2024” at ieeeaic@gmail.com. Always include your paper ID when inquiring about your paper.
